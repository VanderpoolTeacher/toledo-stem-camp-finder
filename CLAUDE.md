# Tech Council — Education Project

## What this is
Working folder for the **Tech Council Education group**. Focus: identifying, documenting, and
conducting **partnership outreach** to **K-12 STEM education providers** (camps, enrichment
programs, school-break programs) in the **Toledo, Ohio metro** area.

## Geographic scope
Toledo + Lucas County and nearby suburbs: **Sylvania, Maumee, Perrysburg, Bowling Green, Oregon**.

## Primary use case
**Partnership outreach** — building contact lists of providers the Tech Council could partner with
or refer. For each provider we capture: organization name, program/STEM focus, location/venue,
contact info (phone, email, website, named contact if available), dates/season, grade levels, and
the source URL.

## Files in this folder
- `Toledo-STEM-Providers-Directory-2026-2027.md` — verified directory of ~11 K-12 STEM providers
  (universities, science centers/zoo/NASA center, community orgs, coding franchises), grouped by
  type, with full contacts and sources. Built via multi-source deep research (June 2026).

## Conventions
- Cite a **source URL** for every provider/claim. Prefer primary/official pages.
- Flag any contact info that could not be verified rather than presenting it as confirmed.
- Note time-sensitivity (program dates/costs/grade bands change yearly — re-verify before outreach).

## Open follow-ups (not yet verified)
Toledo Lucas County Public Library, Boys & Girls Clubs of Toledo, Mathnasium, Engineering for Kids,
and local school district / ESC of Lake Erie West STEM camps — named in scope, no confirmed
offerings surfaced yet. Worth a direct call / second research pass.
