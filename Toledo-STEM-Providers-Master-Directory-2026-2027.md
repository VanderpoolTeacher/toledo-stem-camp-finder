# K-12 STEM Camp & Enrichment Providers — Toledo Metro (2026–2027)
## Master Partnership-Outreach Directory — Tech Council Education Group

**Prepared:** 2026-06-20
**Scope:** K-12 STEM summer camps, school-break, and enrichment programs in the Toledo, OH metro (Lucas County + Sylvania, Maumee, Perrysburg, Bowling Green, Oregon, Holland, Waterville) for the remainder of 2026 and into 2027.
**Method:** Three multi-source deep-research passes (institutional, anchor/gap, and grassroots event-platform) — 67 sources fetched, 75 claims adversarially verified (66 confirmed). All entries from primary/official pages unless noted.
